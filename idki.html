Idukki Wildlife Sanctuary


The sanctuary, flanked on 3 sides by the tranquil waters of Idukki Lake, is housed amidst picturesque settings of tropical evergreen & semi-evergreen forests, meadows, and deciduous trees
Location: Painavu P.O. Idukki–685603

Gavi


Gavi, an eco-tourist spot introduces the visitors to a beguiling scenic beauty that is untouched by the unscrupulous reach of modern humankind. Gavi is home to very rare flora.
Location:28 km from Kumily near Thekkady.

Pothamedu Plantations


Pothamedu Plantations, one of the places to visit in Idukki, bedazzles the tourists with its lush greenery.
Location:6 km from Munnar.

Sita Devi Lake


Another of the Idukki tourist places, Sita Devi Lake, is located at an altitude of 1800 meters (5900 ft) above the sea level. The lake also referred to as Devikulam, which means lake of the Goddess, is supposedly linked to the epic Ramayana.
Location:5 km from Munnar.

Nedumkandam Hills


Nedumkandam Hill, one of the Idukki tourist places, is located at 3200 ft above the sea level at the taluk headquarters of Nedumkandam in Idukki district. The place is popularly known as the land of spices produces coffee, cardamom, and pepper.
Location:40 km from Thekkady.

Cheeyappara Waterfalls


The Cheeyappara Waterfalls is situated on the Kochi-Madurai Highway in Idukki district. The avalanche of water flowing down the seven tiers of the rocky mountain presents a remarkable sight.
Location: Idukki, Kerala.

Kulamavu Dam


Kulamavu Dam, one of the triumvirate dams of the Idukki reservoir project, is constructed in 1961 to restrict the flow of water into Kallivally rivulet.
Location:5 km from Periyar river, Idukki

Malankara Dam


Malankara Dam is built on the Thodupuzha River, which is the tributary of the Muvattupuzha river, for irrigation purpose.
Location:52 km from Kottayam.

Thommankuthu Waterfalls


Thommankuthu Waterfalls is a series of 12 falls or kuthus (locally known) spread over an area of 5 kms and fall from a height of 40 meters
Location:20 km from Thodupuzha

Keezharkuthu Falls


The cascading waters from a height of 1500 meters amidst the hills enveloped in lush greenery, adorns the skies with rainbows that takes the visitors’ breath away.
Location:30 km from Idukki.
