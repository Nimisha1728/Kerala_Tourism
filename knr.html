Palakkayam Thattu


Palakkayam Thattu is one of the alluring places to visit in Kannur near the Western Ghats because of the verdant greenery and dizzying height of about 3350 feet above the sea level. Location:Karuvanchal - Vellad, Chadikundu-Mavumchal, Palakkayam, Thattu, Kerala 670571.

St. Angelo Fort


Considered as one of the most historic sites in Kannur, the St. Angelo Fort was constructed by the first Portuguese viceroy of India. The fort is triangular in shape appears as a massive yet magnificent structure standing at the helm of the ruthless sea. Location:2.0 km from Kannur bus stand.

Payyambalam Beach


The Payyambalam Beach is considered as one of the unspoiled beaches in Kerala, adorned with golden sand and foamy white waters of the Arabian Sea. Location:Payyambalam Beach Walkway, Payyambalam, Kannur, Kerala 670001.

Ezhimala


Perched at a height of 286 meters, the Ezhimala hill is one of the most visually appealing Kannur tourist places.The place also has cultural and historical significance – the place was a battlefield during the Chola-Chera wars. Location:36.0 km from Kannur bus stand.

Dharmadam Island


The untouched shores of the Dharmadam Island offer an escape from routine, worries, and tiredness like never before. Because of its segregation from the hustle and bustle of city life, the island has become a retreat centre for weary travellers. Location:17.2 km from Kannur bus stand.

Paithalmala


Perched at a height of 4500 meters above sea level, this bewitching hill station is located about 65 kms from Kannur and is one of the most amazing places to visit in Kannur. Adrenaline rush seekers will find a haven here – the trek to the village is 6 kms and enlivens up the senses. Location:52 km from Kannur bus stand.

Mapilla Bay


Situated near the famous St Angelo’s fort, the Mapilla Bay a natural yet revamped fishing harbour offering spectacular views of the pristine Arabian Sea waters. The bay holds historical significance because of the Indo-Norwegian pact under which it was modernised. Location:2.1 km from Kannur bus stand.

Madayipara


Spread over a massive 700 acres, the hillock of Madayipura is considered as one of the most famous Kannur tourist places. The gorgeous laterite plateau offers unique and differently coloured hues in every season. Visit the hillock in summer and feast your eyes on the lovely earthy brown and tangerine shades. Location:26.6 km from Kannur bus stand.

Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary


Tropical and evergreen forests stretching over an area of an impressive 55 square meters, the Aralam wildlife sanctuary is truly the finest of its kind. It is truly a wondrous sight to behold – enjoy the exotic array of flora and fauna, catch a glimpse of jumbo elephants and witness the Nilgiri langurs playing on trees. Location:59.2 km from Kannur bus stand.

Muzhappilangad Beach


Famous as Kerala’s only drive-in beach, the Muzhappilangad offers some of the most stunning views of the yellow and orange sun setting along the golden sandy beaches of Malabar coast. Location:13.8 km from Kannur bus stand.
