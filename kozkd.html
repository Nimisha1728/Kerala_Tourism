Pazhassi Raja Museum And Art Gallery


For all the history lovers, Calicut is the perfect place to be. The Pazhassi Raja Museum and Art Gallery is one of the best places to visit in Calicut. Named after the famous ruler Pazhassi Raja who fought the British with the help of tribes, the museum displays a variety of monuments, mural paintings, and old coins. Location:East Hill, Kozhikode, Kerala

Kozhikode Beach


Of all the best beaches Kozhikode is famous for, Calicut beach happens to be an ideal location for tourists who want to unwind themselves from the stress and pollution of the city. Location:1 Km From Railway Station, Kozhikode, India.

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary


The Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary is located among numerous islands near the Kadalundi River that hails from the Arabian Sea. Being a home to both local bird species as well as the migratory birds, this bird sanctuary is indeed one of the mesmerising places to visit during your Calicut sightseeing tour. Location:Near Kadalundi Railway Gate, Kadalundi, Kerala 673302.

Peruvannamuzhi Dam


The dam of Peruvannamuzhi can be seen enveloped within the hills and is considered to be one of the favorite picnic locations for the tourists as well as locals. To make the place more interesting, arrangements for rowboating as well as speed boating have been made. Location:Kozhikode, Calicut.

Kozhippara Falls


The stunning Kozhippara Falls is located on the borders of Kakkadampoyli at the district division of Malappuram and Calicut. You will encounter numerous trekking groups here. The serene ambiance of this falls enhances the beauty of this place. Location:Kozhikode, Kerala.

Beypore


Located in the heart of Calicut, Beypore is a popular ship building port which is known to be one of the busiest fishing harbours of Kerala. Considered to be one of the best places to visit in Calicut, the Beypore beach also happens to be an important commercial center for building the ships. While the city is also famed for its unique shipping vessel called “Uru”, do not miss out on going for a ride on the famous two-kilometer long Pulimoodu bridge. Location:South of Kozhikode Centre, Kozhikode, India.

Thusharagiri Falls


Thusharagiri waterfall is located in the Western Ghats of Kozhikode district. The place is crowded with numerous visitors throughout the year as the fall cascades down as three magnificent waterfalls. Location:Kozhikode, Kerala

Sweet Street


Locally known as Mithai Theravu, Sweet street in Kozhikode is quite popular amongst the tourists and Epicures staying in the town. Location:SM St, Palayam, Kozhikode, Kerala 673001

Kakkayam


This stunning dam site is located in Kozhikode amidst the lush greenery. Location:Kozhikode 673615, India.

Sargaalaya Kerala Arts


If you are interested in witnessing the true art and culture of Kerala while holidaying in Kozhikode then do plan a visit to Sargaalaya Kerala Arts. It is an amazing initiative by the Department of Tourism, Kerala to preserve and spread awareness about the rich art and culture of Kerala. Location:Sargalaya Rd, Iringal, Kerala 673521
